== Description ==

The [Trustruby](https://trustruby.com) plugin lets you easily collect website reviews from customers, as well as show your rating to gain the confidence of your new potential customers. Customer reviews play a crucial role in the customer journey, so displaying the badge on a prominent position during the purchasing process can increase your conversion rate and help you make more sales.

It can also serve as a showcase for other customers about receiving feedback and showing customer care.


Why use TrustRuby for reviews?

*   It's completely free
*   All 7 responsive widgets are free to use and embed on your website
*   Reviews left by anonymous customers have almost no impact on your score
*	It is compatible with WooCommerce

== Installation ==

1. Download the TrustRuby Reviews plugin to your computer.
2. Update and activate the plugin

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is the TrustRuby plugin free? =

Yes, the TrustRuby plugin is completely free.

= How can I reply to the received reviews? =

Simply go to your TrustRuby business profile and write your reply.

= How can integrate the TrustRuby plugin with WooCommerce? =

After you install the plugin, create a shortcode and embed it in your webshop (for example in your checkout template)

= Can I have multiple widgets at the same page? =

Yes, you can embed multiple widgets at the same page.
